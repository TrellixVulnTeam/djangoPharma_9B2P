Our company has been operating in the pharmaceutical sector since 2000. We have been providing pharmaceutical care, health counseling, pharmaceutical, health and beauty products through our Pharmacy Store located at Axarnon 343, Athens.

Our Pharmacy Store was founded in 2000 and has already 17 years of history providing responsible health-care services to the community of Athens. In the period 2000-2014, the Pharmacy operated exclusively under the management of the pharmacist Danai Sotiropoulou.

Our vision is to improve the quality of life of our customers, by providing the best health-care services and products at the best prices, while offering responsible counseling and a pleasant shopping experience.

Our Goal To offer the same services on the Internet, as in our Pharmacy store, to as many people as possible.

We would like to thank all our customers for their trust and loyalty. We promise that will always try to provide them with a respectful, responsible and honest service.
